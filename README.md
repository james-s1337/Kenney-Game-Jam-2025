## [Alien Blast Off!](https://phantom-xyz.itch.io/alien-blast-off)

Welcome fellow alien! Or should I say goodbye?? It seems that you are stuck on a planet full of rogue robots and trying to escape! Not Ideal....

But fret not! Collect the power cells, and you shall power up your spacecraft to make your great escape!

## Overview

Alien Blast Off! is an arcade shooter where you must collect power cells that spawn around the map to power your ship! I have also heard that the power cells can give you some power ups too...

Gameplay Overview:

- Colelct power cells
- Defend your spaceship
- Get powerups from power cells
- Escape! Or keep fighting!

## Controls

A+D - Move

W - Jump

Space - Fire
